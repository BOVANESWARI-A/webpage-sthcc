Admission Procedure

APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION

Application for admission to U.G. Courses and P.G Courses can be obtained from the college website only.

Application and Registration Fee

UG Courses: Rs 50 & PG Courses: Rs 60

IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS

  • For admission to U.G. Courses, preference will be given to those who are aged above 17 years and below 21 years and who have creditably passed all the examinations in the first attempt.
  • Admission to B.Sc degree courses is restricted to those who have studied the relevant subjects at the HSC level.
  • For admission to B.Com., preference will be given to those who have studied Mathematics, Accountancy, Commerce, and Economics at the HSC level (Academic Stream).
  • For Undergraduate courses, students should opt for anyone of the extension activities viz NCC, NSS, Red Cross, Youth Welfare.
  • The eligibility criteria for admission to P.G. Science courses is 40% and above marks in part III subjects for Scheduled Castes and Tribes and 45% and above for other communities.
  • College Timing: 8.30 a.m. to 1.30 p.m.
  • Dress Code: Uniforms for all students except M.Phil and Ph.D. Scholars.
  • Admission of candidates other than those who have passed TN Higher Secondary Examination is purely provisional, subject to the approval of the University.
  • Students who are found to have obtained admission by false representation shall be summarily dismissed with forfeiture of whatever fees they have paid.

CONDITIONS FOR ADMISSION

  • Admission will be made through Counseling.
  • The upper age limit for admission to U.G. courses will be 21 as on 1-7-2020. Relaxation to the SC / ST / BC / MBC / DNC / Women and Transgender candidates – 3 years; Physically handicapped candidates 5 years.
  • Xerox copies of the following certificates to be enclosed along with the filled-in Application Form:
    • Qualifying Examination Mark Sheet (Three attested copies).
    • Community Certificate (Two attested copies).
    • Certificate of Proof when admission is sought under some special quota / concessions-like Physically Handicapped / Sports / Ex- Serviceman / NCC etc.
    • Income Certificate.
  • For admission to B.Com., preference will be given to those who have studied Mathematics, Accountancy, Commerce, and Economics at the HSC level (Academic Stream).
  • Application for admission to U.G. / P.G. courses should be registered in the college office within 10 days from the date of publication of the latest result of the Higher Secondary / M.S. University degree examinations. The registration slip should be produced for all enquiries regarding application.
  • Interview Card bearing the requisite postage stamp with the full address of the applicant must be submitted with the application.
  • They will have to appear with their parent/guardian before the Principal on the date and time of counseling mentioned in the letter of Counseling. They must produce at the time of the interview all documents specified in the interview card in original.
  • If the selection is confirmed, the prescribed fees must be paid immediately after the interview, failing which the candidate will lose the seat. Students belonging to Scheduled Castes / Tribes, M.B.C. and Denotified Community need not remit tuition fee and special fee at the time of admission, if they produce the relevant certificate. Their fees will be deducted from the scholarship.
  • Girl Students (P.G.) whose parental annual income does not exceed Rs.50,000/- need not remit tuition fee if they produce the Income Certificate.
  • If there is any grievance in the matter of admission, it may be brought to the notice of the Joint Director of Collegiate Education, Thirunelveli.
  • N.B. Incomplete Application Forms will be summarily rejected.

RULE OF RESERVATION

  • The admission shall be made purely on the basis of Merit subject to the rule of reservation of the Government of Tamil Nadu.
  • 31% for Open Competition (OC).
  • 20% for Most Backward Class (MBC) and Denotified Communities (DNC).
  • 30% for Backward Classes (BC) within this 3.5% for Muslims.
  • In the 18% for Scheduled Castes (SC), 3% of seats are offered to Aranthathiyars (within the seats reserved for S.C).
  • 1% for Scheduled tribes (ST).
